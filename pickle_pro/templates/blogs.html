Homemade mango pickle is a traditional Indian delicacy made using raw mangoes, spices, and oil.

To prepare it, raw mangoes are washed, chopped, and sun-dried to remove moisture. They are then mixed with mustard seeds, fenugreek, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and asafoetida. The mixture is soaked in mustard oil, sealed in a jar, and kept in sunlight for several days to enhance the flavor. This tangy and spicy pickle adds a delicious touch to any meal. It can be stored for months and is enjoyed with rice, chapati, or paratha. Making it at home ensures purity and taste.
